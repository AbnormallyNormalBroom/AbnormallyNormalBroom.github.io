Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    Go to print.hz.nl, upload your file and print it at one of the printers at HZ using your student pass

  • How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event at Friday 16/09) on the HZ web shop?

    Go to webshop.hz.nl select the item of choice buy it.

  • Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

    Here you can find information regarding health problems.

  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    Go to the selfservice portal module at hz.nl, choose "make a reservation", then "Reserve meeting room" and book the room of choice.

  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    Free parking is available at the car park of PZEM at Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg.
