Companies of interest
As of right now, the profession of software engineer sounds like an interesting profession, and with that come many potential workplaces in a big variety of companies. Companies with huge reach and others with less reach but of equal importance.

one of those companies with big reach is of course Microsoft. This company is of interest to me because it is a leader in so many different parts of the ICT industry. Most computers run on their operating systems, The most common tools of the pc, word, PowerPoint, excel etc. are made by Microsoft. They even have a solid place in the gaming industry with their XBOX consoles.

Other companies with less reach but no less importance would be Nvidia, AMD and Intel, because so many computers run on their GPU’s and CPU’s, even apple’s MacBooks have intel chips inside. While companies as Microsoft and Google are trying to conquer as many fields of the internet as possible, these companies are developing new technologies and making new things.

There are also companies that make things that are very new to the general public. Something such as Virtual Reality is a very new concept that has a lot of potential. Companies as Oculus or Pimax and many others are making headsets, companies are even researching and building haptic gloves and suits that make you feel things as walls and punches that aren’t really there. I would love to get a job as software developer in one of these companies, because they are exploring and building something completely different than the ordinary computer.

Ofcourse there are a lot more interesting companies that relate in some way with Software engineering, but these companies have the most important roles which makes them really interesting and very considerable workplaces.
